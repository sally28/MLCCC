MLCCC Weekend School Classes

Serving children and adults (ages 3 and up), the MLCCC Weekend School provides a rich assortment of language and cultural classes that support attendees' educational, personal, and career goals. These classes include:


Mandarin Chinese Language

Preliminary Mandarin Classes

  • Montessori Chinese for Pre-K (3 yrs to 5 yrs)

    • This is a 50-minute class geared toward younger children both with or without Mandarin speaking environment at home. The curriculum is theme based and tailored to teach students speaking, understanding, and reading through many hands-on manipulatives. Using teaching techniques like circle time, individual time, guided play and games, these young ones will have fun together and learn at the same time.

  • Montessori Chinese Kindergarten (4 yrs to 6 yrs)

    • This is a 100-minute class (two 50 minutes with 10 minutes break) geared toward kindergarten age children. We strongly suggest children with limited Chinese exposure to join this class. The curriculums are theme based and tailored to teach students speaking, understanding, reading, and writing through many hands-on manipulatives. Using teaching techniques like circle time, individual time, guided play and games, the students will have fun together and learn at the same time.

  • Chinese as a Heritage Language Kindergartenn

    • This is a 100-minute class (two 50 minutes with a 10 minute break) for kindergarten age children, using the textbook 美洲华语 MeiZhouHuaYu (简体版 simplified characters version)

Chinese as Heritage language (CHL) Intensive Track (马立平 Ma Liping textbooks, grades 1 - high school)

The CHL Intensive program is for students who have a Mandarin Chinese-speaking environment at home, who already have strong Chinese language abilities, especially in understanding and speaking, who will have time to review and challenge themselves. The recommended amount of homework and study time is 40 minutes a day for at least 4-5 times per week. It is also recommended that parents assist the students in their studies.

The suggested age to start this program is 6 years old in grade 1. This program teaches how to speak, read, and write Mandarin every academic year as the student advances from grade 1 through high school.

The 1st to 10th grade CHL program uses 马立平 Ma Liping textbooks (简体版 simplified characters version) as curriculums. This curriculum has the following characteristics:

  • Learn Chinese characters first, then learn Pinyin
  • Learn how to recognize Chinese characters first, then learn how to write the characters
  • Learn Chinese reading first, then learn Chinese writing
  • Using stories that are fun and well liked by children
  • Use multimedia (eg. CD-ROM) as part of homework to make the assignments more enjoyable for students.

The recommended age to start this program is 6 years old

Chinese as a Heritage Language Regular Track (美洲华语MeiZhouHuaYu textbooks, grades 1 - high school)

The CHL Regular program is for students who have a Mandarin Chinese-speaking environment at home, who already understand and speak some Chinese, but who does not have much time to review. The recommended amount of homework and study time is 30 minutes a day for at least 2 times per week.

The suggested age to start this program is 6 years old in grade 1. This program runs from grade 1 through high school, and uses the 美洲华语 MeiZhouHuaYu textbooks (简体版 simplified characters version) as curriculum.

CSL - Chinese As a Second Language (multiple levels for children, teens and adults)

The CSL program is for students who do not have a Mandarin Chinese-speaking environment at home, and who cannot communicate using basic Mandarin. We offer multiple levels based on Mandarin ability.

AP Chinese / SAT II Chinese

This class prepares students for the AP Chinese and SAT II Chinese exams.
